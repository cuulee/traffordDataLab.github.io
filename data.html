Data

We are proactively gathering and publishing data owned by Trafford Council and its partners. The data (and metadata) is signposted on the DataGM website. This is to ensure that open data are discoverable and accessible. Datasets in DataGM about Trafford by the Lab include:

  • Defibrillators. Locations of automated external defibrillators (AEDs).
  • Blue Plaques. Locations of blue or heritage plaques with details.
  • Jobseekers Allowance Claimants. Number and percentage of jobseekers allowance claimants.
  • Number of Red1 responses by North West Ambulance Service in Trafford. Dataset created to support the rollout of defibrillators project in Trafford.
  • Grants to voluntary and community organisations - Trafford. Dataset of grants allocated by Trafford Council via Trafford Partnership to voluntary and community organisations.
