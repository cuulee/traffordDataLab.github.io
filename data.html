Data

We are proactively gathering and publishing data owned by Trafford Council and its partners. The data (and metadata) can be found in our open_data repo on GitHub. We publish our data as 3 star open data as a minimum and provide it in CSV, JSON and GeoJSON formats as appropriate. Datasets available include:

  • Betting shops. Dataset for betting shop locations within Greater Manchester derived from the Gambling Commission public register of premises licence data.
  • Claimant count and rates. The claimant count measures the number of people claiming unemployment related benefits. The claimant rate measures the proportion of residents aged 16-64 who are claimants. From April 2015 the count includes all Universal Credit claimants who are required to seek work as well as all JSA claimants.
  • Defibrillators. Approximate locations of automated external defibrillators (AEDs).
  • Employment and support allowance benefit claimants. Employment and Support Allowance (ESA) replaced Incapacity Benefit and Income Support paid on the grounds of incapacity for new claims from 27th October 2008.
  • GP Practices. Name and address of GP Practices.
  • Grants. Dataset of grants allocated by Trafford Council via Trafford Partnership to voluntary and community organisations.
  • English Indices of Deprivation 2015. The Indices of Deprivation 2015 provide a set of relative measures of deprivation for Lower Super Output Areas based on seven domains of deprivation.
  • Jobcentre Plus offices. The address and coordinates of all Jobcentre Plus offices in Greater Manchester.
  • Lone parent households with dependent children. Number of households with lone parent not in employment.
  • Ward level mid-year population estimates. Estimates of the usual resident population for 2016 electoral wards in Greater Manchester.
  • No qualifications. Count of working-age adults with no qualifications.
  • Social rented households. Tenure table classifies households by tenure. The present dataset extracts households classified as social rented.
