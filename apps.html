Apps

The Lab produce interactive web applications using the Shiny R package. The apps are designed to allow users to interactively interrogate the data and also provide links to the raw data.

November 2017

English indices of deprivation 2015 for Greater Manchester
Visualising the data released by the Department for Communities and Local Government for Lower-layer Super Output Areas (LSOA) in Greater Manchester.


October 2017

Trafford Road Casuality Map
Interactive visualisation of road casualty data recorded by Greater Manchester Police during 2016.
